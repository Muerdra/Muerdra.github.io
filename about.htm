The Big Three

ANIME

Since my highschool days I've been a casual watcher of anime. I have a huge respect for animation and fantasy. An old friend lent me one of the first anime's I ever watched, My-Hime.

After watching that anime, I immediately watched the second season and have since watched perhaps a hundred since then with no stop in sight.


DRAWING

Drawing has been a vent and relaxation for me. I've only started to try and learn how to better myself through videos and books (on and off the web).


GAMING

PC Gaming for me was quite an experience. From my early days of StarCraft/BroodWar, WarCraft II, Total Annihilation, Total Annihilation Kingdoms and Age of Empires/II.

Evolving into my love of turn-based fantasy with a game called Age of Wonders 2. I've since branched into card games like Shadowverse. A little franchise called Dawn of War and Warframe.


Throughout my years I've played many a game and delved into their universes. But the two that have earned a place in my hall-of-fame are Warframe and Warhammer Both Warframe and Warhammer 40k have shaped myself and influenced the world of my story (not all things are sunshine and rainbows). And of course I had to draw the Imperium of Man's finest, Grey Knight.
