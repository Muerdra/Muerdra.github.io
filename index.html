A MYSTERIOUS DISCOVERY

We discovered Toha Island by accident while performing an expedition through the Pacific Ocean. Our boat had been mysteriously drawn in and placed on the shore of Toha Island during the night. We awoke to the sound of strange creatures inhabiting the island.

ISLAND ENVIRONMENT

Toha Island has a very bizarre topography. While it is only 10000 km², it has eight active volcanoes, a very lush jungle, a small desert and a mountain range.
